German Test Rules

You need to score 200 points to pass your German test before
the summer holidays. So far, you have already collected 150 points due
to prior assignments. To collect the rest of the 150 points, you need
to do this final test and collect German words associated with holiday.
and avoid German words associated with study.
You get 1 point for each German holiday word you collect.
You get -1 point for each German study word you pick wrongly.
You fail if you reach 0 points.
If you pass the test, you get to go on your summer holiday = )
If you fail the test, you must stay back during summer for extra
German classes = (
